FAQ

  • How do I enroll in online banking?

    To enroll in online banking, simply visit our website and click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" button. Follow the prompts to provide your account information, create a username and password, and complete the registration process. You'll then have access to our online banking services.

  • How do I reset my online banking password?

    If you forget your online banking password, you can click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to verify your identity, and you'll be prompted to create a new password.

  • How can I transfer money to another bank account?

    With our online banking platform, you can easily transfer funds to another bank account. Simply log in to your account, navigate to the "Transfer" or "Payments" section, and follow the steps to provide the recipient's account details and the amount you wish to transfer.

  • Can I view my account statements online?

    Absolutely! Through our online banking platform, you can access and view your account statements digitally. Simply log in to your account, navigate to the "Statements" or "Documents" section, and select the desired statement to review or download.
